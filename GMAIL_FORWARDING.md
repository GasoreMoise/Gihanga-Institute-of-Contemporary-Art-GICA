# How to Forward Emails from Gmail to contact@gica.art

## Option 1: Gmail Forwarding (Easiest)

### In Gmail (gasorenshuti34@gmail.com):

1. Go to **Settings** (gear icon) → **See all settings**
2. Click **"Forwarding and POP/IMAP"** tab
3. Click **"Add a forwarding address"**
4. Enter: `contact@gica.art`
5. Click **"Next"** → **"Proceed"**
6. Check your `contact@gica.art` inbox for confirmation email
7. Click the confirmation link
8. Go back to Gmail settings
9. Select **"Forward a copy of incoming mail to"** → Choose `contact@gica.art`
10. Choose what to do with Gmail copy: **"Keep Gmail's copy"**
11. Click **"Save Changes"**

Now all emails sent to `gasorenshuti34@gmail.com` will automatically forward to `contact@gica.art`!

---

## Option 2: Use Filters (Advanced)

1. In Gmail, go to Settings → **"Filters and Blocked Addresses"**
2. Click **"Create new filter"**
3. Set criteria: `To: gasorenshuti34@gmail.com`
4. Click **"Create filter"**
5. Check **"Forward it to:"** → Enter `contact@gica.art`
6. Click **"Create filter"**

---

## Option 3: Domain Provider Email Forwarding

If your client has domain management access:

1. Go to domain provider dashboard
2. Set up email forwarding: `contact@gica.art` → forwards to → `gasorenshuti34@gmail.com`
3. Then reverse forward in Gmail: `gasorenshuti34@gmail.com` → forwards to → `contact@gica.art`

---

## Test It!

After setting up forwarding:

1. Send a test email to `gasorenshuti34@gmail.com`
2. Check `contact@gica.art` - you should receive it
3. If it works, the contact form emails will reach `contact@gica.art` automatically!
